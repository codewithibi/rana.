Entrepreneurship Journey

My passion for entrepreneurship started early and continues to shape how I approach challenges and opportunities.

Teaching Entrepreneurship

I manage a young entrepreneur class where I explain Robert T. Kiyosaki's "Rich Dad's Cashflow Quadrant" principles to my peers. These sessions focus on understanding different income types and building financial intelligence at a young age.

My Teaching Curriculum

  • Session 1
    Understanding Income Quadrants

    Introduction to the four quadrants: Employee, Self-employed, Business owner, and Investor

  • Session 2
    Moving from E to B Quadrant

    Strategies for transitioning from employee mindset to business owner thinking

  • Session 3
    Financial Intelligence

    Building fundamental understanding of assets vs. liabilities for young entrepreneurs

  • Session 4
    Cash Flow Management

    Essential principles of managing money effectively for business success
